Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is an iconic American-style pizza featuring a crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce,
melted mozzarella cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices. When baked, the pepperoni curls into tiny cups, releasing
flavorful oils that crisp at the edges. It’s often garnished with Parmesan, oregano, or red pepper flakes for extra kick.

Ingredients

  • Dough Ingredients:
    • 2½ cups (300g) all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp instant yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 cup (240ml) warm water (110°F/45°C)
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Toppings:
    • ½ cup (120ml) pizza sauce (or marinara)
    • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 30-40 slices pepperoni
    • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
    • ½ tsp dried oregano (optional)
    • Red pepper flakes (for serving)

Step-by-Step

  1. Make the dough:
    • In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
    • Knead for 5 mins (by hand or mixer) until smooth. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour (or until doubled).
  2. Preheat & prep:
    • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, heat it in the oven.
    • Punch down the dough. Roll it out on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a parchment-lined tray.
  3. Assemble:
    • Spread sauce evenly, leaving a ½-inch border. Sprinkle mozzarella, then add pepperoni (slightly overlapping). Top with Parmesan and oregano.
  4. Bake:
    • Bake for 12-15 mins until crust is golden and cheese bubbles. For extra crispiness, broil for 1-2 mins at the end.
  5. Serve:
    • Slice and serve hot with red pepper flakes!
