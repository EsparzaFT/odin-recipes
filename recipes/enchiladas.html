Enchiladas Suizas

Description

Enchiladas Suizas ("Swiss-style enchiladas") are a creamy, cheesy Mexican dish featuring rolled corn tortillas
filled with shredded chicken, smothered in a tangy tomatillo-green chili sauce, and baked under a blanket of
melted cheese (typically Oaxaca or Monterey Jack).

Ingredients

  • For the sauce (Salsa Verde):
    • 1 lb (500g) tomatillos, husked & rinsed
    • 1-2 serrano peppers (adjust for spice)
    • 1/2 white onion, chopped
    • 2 garlic cloves
    • 1/2 cup cilantro
    • 1 cup chicken broth
    • 1/2 cup Mexican crema (or heavy cream)
    • Salt to taste
  • For the filling:
    • 1/2 cup diced onions
    • 1 tbsp oil
  • Assembly:
    • 12 corn tortillas
    • 2 cups shredded Oaxaca cheese (or Monterey Jack)
    • 1/4 cup cotija cheese (optional, for topping)
    • Mexican crema & cilantro for garnish

Step-by-Step

  1. Make the salsa verde:
    • Boil tomatillos, serranos, onion, and garlic in water for 10 mins until soft. Drain.
    • Blend with cilantro, broth, and crema until smooth. Season with salt.
  2. Sauté the filling:
    • Heat oil in a pan. Cook onions until translucent, then add chicken to warm through.
  3. Soft-fry tortillas:
    • Lightly fry tortillas in oil (10 sec/side) to make them pliable. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Assemble & bake:
    • Dip each tortilla in salsa verde, fill with chicken, roll, and place seam-side down in a baking dish.
    • Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas, top with cheese, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 mins until bubbly.
  5. Garnish & serve:
    • Drizzle with crema, sprinkle cotija and cilantro. Serve with refried beans or Mexican rice!
